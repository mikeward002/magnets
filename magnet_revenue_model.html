Sariah went from 0 to $100K/month in 6 months primarily via TikTok + Instagram viral content — no paid ads until after she was already generating significant revenue. The content IS the marketing. Every magnet-making video, wedding highlight, and "pack orders with me" is a conversion funnel.

Volume play labor warning: The honest trade-off at $4.50/magnet is that you need ~1,700 D2C orders/month to hit $1M/year (net of weddings). At – hrs/order that's – hours/day just in D2C production + fulfillment — that's a full-time employee before you even do weddings or social. The math only works solo up to about 600–700 orders/month. Above that, you need help, which is totally fine if you've modeled it.
The compounding weddings strategy: Every wedding you do is content. A single wedding video of guests getting their magnets gets 50K–500K views. Those views convert to D2C orders, wedding booking inquiries, and eventually kit buyer interest. Sariah's growth loop: wedding video → viral → more D2C orders + more wedding bookings → revenue → more content. This is why the wedding channel is the seed of everything, not just a revenue line.
